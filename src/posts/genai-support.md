# Building GenAI Support Experiences at Dell

At Dell, I’m part of the team building the backend orchestration behind the GenAI-powered Dell Support experience.

## What the system does

- Connects multiple LLMs into a single, deterministic workflow for support responses.
- Uses Neo4j Knowledge Graphs and vector databases to ground every response in real product data.
- Applies Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) so answers stay accurate and free from hallucination risk.
- Runs as scalable Python microservices that keep latency low even under high support volume.

## My focus

I design prompt pipelines, retrieval systems, and agentic orchestration layers that turn complex support workflows into reliable, context-aware automation.
